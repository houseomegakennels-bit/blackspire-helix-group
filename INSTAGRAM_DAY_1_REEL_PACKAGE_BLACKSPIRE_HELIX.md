# Instagram Day 1 Reel Package for `@blackspirehelixgroup`

Date: June 1, 2026

## Goal

Introduce the account clearly and make people understand what `Blackspire Helix` does in under 10 seconds.

## Best Format

Use a short Reel:

- length: `8 to 15 seconds`
- style: cinematic AI visuals or sharp motion graphics
- pacing: fast
- text-led: yes
- voiceover: optional

## Core Message

`Blackspire Helix builds AI employees that automate growth, leads, and operations 24/7.`

1. `Your next employee might be AI.`
2. `We build AI employees for real businesses.`
3. `What if your business had AI staff working 24/7?`
4. `Growth, leads, and ops. Automated.`

- use high-contrast captions
