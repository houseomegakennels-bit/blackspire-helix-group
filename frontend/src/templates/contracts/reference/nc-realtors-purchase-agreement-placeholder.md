# NC REALTORS Purchase Agreement Placeholder

Source URL: https://www.ncrealtors.org/legal-ethics/forms-contracts/document-library/

Intended purpose: Used between the seller and the acquisition entity to place the property under contract in North Carolina.

Required fields:
- seller_name
- buyer_or_acquisition_entity_name
- property_address
- purchase_price
- earnest_money_deposit
- closing_date
- inspection_period
- title_company
- special_terms
