## Reel Rules

Follow these every time:

- hook in the first 1 to 2 seconds
- keep the frame vertical and clean
- use readable on-screen text
- avoid cluttered captions
- do not recycle content with visible third-party watermarks
- favor short, rewatchable clips over slow intros
- every Reel should do one job:
  - get attention
  - build authority
  - or convert profile visitors into followers

## Caption Formula

Keep captions short.

Format:

1. one-line hook
2. one-line meaning or payoff
3. one CTA

Example:

`What if your next employee was an AI system that never sleeps?`

`This is the kind of automation we build for growth, leads, and operations.`

`Follow @blackspirehelixgroup for more AI systems and visuals.`

## CTA Formula

Rotate these:

- `Follow for daily AI systems and visuals`
- `DM "AI" if you want this built for your business`
- `Follow for more automation ideas`

## Post Review Checklist

Before posting, check:

1. is the first second visually strong?
2. is the on-screen text readable without sound?
3. is the message clear in one sentence?
4. is there a reason to follow?
5. is the CTA obvious?

## What To Track

After each Reel, track:

- views
- watch retention if available
- shares
- saves
- profile visits
- follows from the post

If a Reel gets profile visits but low follows, improve the profile.

If a Reel gets views but low profile visits, improve the hook/message match.

If a Reel gets strong watch time, remake it in 2 to 3 variants.
